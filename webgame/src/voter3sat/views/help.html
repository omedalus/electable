• You must convince every voter to vote for you.
  • Each voter has a strong opinion about three issues.
  • A voter will vote for you if they agree with you on at least one of their three personal issues.
  • Flip your stances on an issue to switch which voters agree/disagree with you on it.
  • A winning combination of stances will match at least one personal issue of every voter.
  • You can only make a limited number of flipflops, so choose carefully!
